University Nova

At University of Nova, we believe in the transformative power of education and the boundless potential within every individual. Established in 2026, we have been dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity, academic excellence, and a vibrant campus community.

Our diverse programs across arts, sciences, business, and technology attract students from over 80 countries, creating a truly global learning environment.
