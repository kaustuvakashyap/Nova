Key Deadlines

Critical submission windows and registration dates for the 2026–27 academic year. Mark these in your planner well in advance.

1 Sep 2026

Course Add / Drop Deadline

Final date to modify Semester One enrolment without academic penalty.

10 Oct 2026

Exam Registration Closes

All students must register for end-of-semester examinations by this date.

15 Oct 2026

Tuition Fee Payment — Rs.15,000

Semester One fees due in full. Payment plans must be arranged before this date.

26 Feb 2027

Dissertation Proposals

PG research students must submit approved proposals to supervisors.

28 Mar 2027

Convocation Registration

Graduating students must confirm attendance for the Spring ceremony.

15 Feb 2027

Tuition Fee Payment — Rs.16,000

Semester Two fees due. Late payment incurs an administrative surcharge.
