Our Story

A Legacy Built on
Curiosity & Courage

"Nova was born from a simple conviction: that the pursuit of knowledge, married to moral purpose, can change the world."

The University of Nova was established in 2026 by a coalition of scholars and civic leaders who believed higher education should be bold, inclusive, and relentlessly forward-looking. What began as a single faculty of sciences on a leafy urban campus has grown into one of the most respected research universities in the hemisphere.

Today, Nova spans eight faculties, hundreds of research laboratories, and a global network of partner institutions. Our students come from over 80 countries, drawn by our commitment to rigorous scholarship, ethical leadership, and transformative discovery.

Our Mission & Core Values

Academic Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of scholarship, rigorous inquiry, and intellectual honesty — in every classroom and every laboratory.

02

Global Citizenship

We nurture graduates who think beyond borders — equipped with cross-cultural fluency and a deep sense of responsibility to the global community.

Nova is home to every background, belief, and perspective. Diversity is not a goal — it is the very foundation of brilliant thinking.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Nova

Founded by Visionaries

The University of Nova opens its doors with 400 students, one faculty of sciences, and a founding promise: to make excellence available to all.

School of Law & Medicine

Two landmark faculties are established, doubling enrolment and earning the university's first national accreditation from the Higher Education Council.

First International Campus

Nova opens a satellite campus in Geneva, formalizing a global research network that now spans 40 cities across six continents.

QS Top 200 Ranking

For the first time, Nova enters the QS World University Rankings, debuting at #187 — the fastest rise by any institution in that decade.

Nova Innovation Hub Launch

The 40,000 sq ft Nova Innovation Hub opens, becoming a launchpad for 60+ startups, three of which have since reached unicorn status.

Carbon-Neutral Campus

Nova achieves full carbon neutrality across its main campus, becoming one of only twelve universities globally to reach this milestone ahead of schedule.
