Step One

Admission Requirements

Before applying, ensure you have the following documents ready. All submissions are made digitally through our secure student portal.

Undergraduate Secondary school certificate or equivalent
Postgraduate Accredited bachelor's degree (min. 2.5 GPA)
International Equivalent qualifications with certified translation
01

Official Transcripts

All academic transcripts from secondary school and above. Documents must be certified copies issued directly by the institution, bearing an official seal or stamp.

Must be submitted in English or with a certified translation
02

Passport / National ID

A clear, high-resolution scan of the photo/bio-data page of your valid passport or government-issued national identity document. Expired documents will not be accepted.

File must be in PDF, JPG, or PNG format (min. 300 dpi)
03

English Proficiency Certificate

Required for all non-native English speakers. Accepted tests: IELTS (min. 6.5 overall), TOEFL iBT (min. 85), or Cambridge C1 Advanced / C2 Proficiency. Must be within 2 years.

Native speakers or those from English-medium schools may be exempt
04

Additional Documents

Supporting materials that strengthen your application — academic awards, published works, creative portfolios, or letters of recommendation from teachers, employers, or mentors.

Up to 5 supporting documents; portfolio max 20MB
06

Application Fee Receipt

Proof of payment for the non-refundable application processing fee. Fee waivers are available for students applying under financial hardship — contact our admissions office.

Domestic: $50 | International: $75
Step Two

The Admission Process

A clear, structured pathway from your first click to your first day on campus. Our team supports you at every stage.

Register on the Nova Student Portal using a valid email address. You'll receive a confirmation link to activate your account and access the application dashboard.

Go to Portal

Fill out the online application form and upload all required documents. Ensure all files meet our format and size specifications. Incomplete applications will not be reviewed.

Our academic committee evaluates your application against program-specific criteria. You'll receive an email acknowledging receipt within 3 business days of submission.

Select programs require a personal interview with faculty members. Shortlisted candidates are notified by email and given a scheduling link for a time that suits them.

Successful applicants receive a formal offer letter via email and through the portal. You'll have 21 days to accept your offer and pay the enrollment deposit to secure your place.

Complete your enrollment formalities, receive your student ID, select your modules, and attend Nova's Welcome Week — your launchpad into campus life.
