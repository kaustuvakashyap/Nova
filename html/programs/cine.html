The Motion Pictures track offers a Bachelor of Arts in Cinematography. Engineered for visual stylists, this professional module analyzes mechanical sensor behaviors, photometric configurations, and advanced color matrices to develop image directors capable of steering world-class sets.

About The Program

Students selecting the Cinematography discipline enter directly into active studio environments, mastering advanced lighting designs, mechanical crane maneuvers, and technical optics. Mentored by active members of global film guilds, master camera operators, and elite post-production artists, you will decode the exact science behind framing emotion and structuring shadows.

Our soundstages maintain strong strategic pipelines with industry equipment manufacturers. Majors benefit from practical, high-stakes film productions, cross-departmental collaboration with screenwriting labs, and competitive festival distribution support that turns student thesis films into professional statements.
