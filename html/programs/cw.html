The Creative Writing program offers a Master of Fine Arts (MFA) in Contemporary Letters. The curriculum is structured around intensive workshop groups, historical analysis, and structural narrative execution, offering writers a critical forum to refine their unique voice while preparing for competitive global publishing and multimedia industries.

About The Program

Students entering the Creative Writing program step into a historic tradition of formal experimentation and structural craftsmanship. Working under internationally published novelists, acclaimed essayists, and award-winning poets, you will analyze narrative structures, explore cross-genre experimentation, and master the technical mechanics of pacing, dialogue, and voice.

Nova University is affiliated with prominent literary reviews and independent presses. Program majors benefit from structured mentorship paths, professional manuscript reviews, and local community outreach programs that emphasize storytelling as an essential force for cultural preservation and dynamic social dialogue.
