The Spatial Arts track offers a Bachelor of Science in Architecture Design. This intensive, studio-centric program merges ecological parameter frameworks, spatial sociology research, and robust building Information modeling (BIM) methods to challenge future architects to engineer resilient communities.

About The Program

Students electing to enter the Architecture studio engage directly with parametric workflows, experimental building materials, and site-specific topography design. Guided by globally recognized urban design practitioners, structural experts, and environment researchers, you will discover how architectural configurations can actively respond to environmental shifts.

Nova University's design labs are deeply connected with regional construction innovation groups. Students gain vital exposure through large-scale community installations, design-build research initiatives, and public presentations that balance aesthetic form with strict socio-economic and structural accountability metrics.
